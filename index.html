English

This Privacy Policy describes how the MedCalc+ application ("the App") handles information. The App is intended exclusively for use by qualified healthcare professionals as a clinical decision support tool.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, health data, or identifiable information. All calculations are performed locally on your device. Any data you enter into calculators is not saved, transmitted, or shared, and is discarded when you close the calculator.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process certain data (such as device identifiers, approximate location, and app usage) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This data is collected by Google, not by us. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data.

Children

The App is not directed at children and is intended for healthcare professionals only.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
